Interdisciplinary Neuroscience and Clinical Research Initiative

This site documents the shared vision, scientific architecture, and collaborative roadmap for integrative neurodiagnostics and clinical research.

Overview

The platform unifies neuroscience research, clinical diagnostics, and open knowledge practices to accelerate translational impact. This page provides an orientation to current priorities and reference materials.

Current Highlights

Scientific Scope Update

Refined frameworks for neuroimaging biomarkers and multimodal signal integration are in review. See the Scientific Scope section for detailed documentation.

Clinical Pathway Alignment

Preliminary clinician feedback sessions completed across neurology and psychiatry departments. Clinical integration guidelines will be published in Q2.

Governance Milestone

Ethics advisory board charter ratified with interdisciplinary oversight. Governance documents are accessible in the Ethics & Governance section.

Next Actions

  • Publish beta documentation on knowledge architecture and data standards.
  • Complete accessibility audit of clinical interface prototypes.
  • Prepare open science collaboration guidelines and submission processes.
