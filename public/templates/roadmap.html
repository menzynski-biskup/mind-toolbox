Research and Platform Roadmap

This roadmap aligns scientific inquiry, clinical integration, and platform engineering milestones over a three-year horizon.

Milestone Timeline

2025 Q1 · Foundation Alignment

Status: Complete · Owner: Program Management Office

Established cross-disciplinary governance charter, completed initial clinical partner interviews, and defined data stewardship requirements.

2025 Q3 · Data Architecture Prototype

Status: In progress · Owner: Data Architecture Lead

Deliver first iteration of unified data model, define ingestion workflows, and validate ontology mappings with scientific leads.

2026 Q1 · Clinician Pilot

Status: Planned · Owner: Clinical Integration Team

Launch limited pilot with clinical partners, evaluate neurodiagnostic workflows, and collect feedback on decision-support interfaces.

Work Packages

WP1 · Scientific Frameworks

Curate interdisciplinary protocols covering neuroimaging, electrophysiology, and cognitive assessment methods.

WP2 · Clinical Integration

Design clinician-facing workflows, develop guardrails for patient safety, and ensure regulatory compliance.

WP3 · Platform Engineering

Implement static-first architecture, data services, and API-ready infrastructure with rigorous security baselines.
