Clinical Scope

Clinical workflows, integration priorities, and patient safeguards supporting responsible deployment.

Review cadence · Quarterly Owner · Clinical Advisory Group

Diagnostic Pathways

  1. Referral intake and eligibility screening with structured consent confirmation.
  2. Neurodiagnostic data acquisition following protocol-specific standard operating procedures.
  3. Integrated analysis review with interdisciplinary case conference.
  4. Clinical recommendation issuance, documentation, and follow-up schedule.

Clinical Integration Focus

  • Embed decision-support summaries into electronic health record systems via standards-based APIs.
  • Provide clinician training modules with accessibility features and evidence references.
  • Maintain outcome tracking dashboards for longitudinal efficacy assessment.

Patient Safeguards

All clinical deployments require dual approval from the Governance Board and institutional review boards, with continuous monitoring of consent status and data minimisation.

Consent Lifecycle
Dynamic consent with participant portal planned for 2026 release.
Data Security
Encryption in transit and at rest, access logging, and least privilege policies.
Clinical Escalation
Rapid escalation pathway to specialty clinicians for adverse findings.
