Publications and Preprints

Planned repository for peer-reviewed outputs, preprints, and citation metadata.

Status · Planned Target launch · 2026 Q2

Planned Structure

  • Peer-reviewed publications indexed with DOI, journal metadata, and accessible summaries.
  • Preprint listings with links to repositories and change history.
  • Supplementary materials including datasets, analysis notebooks, and replication packages.

Governance Approach

Outputs undergo internal review prior to publication. Citation standards follow Vancouver style with persistent identifiers for datasets and software.

Upcoming Enhancements

  1. Automated ingestion from institutional repositories and ORCID records.
  2. Interactive filters for modality, cohort, and clinical relevance.
  3. API endpoints exposing bibliographic metadata for partner integration.
