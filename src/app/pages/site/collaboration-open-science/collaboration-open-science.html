Partnership Models

  • Academic Collaborations: Joint research agendas with shared governance representation.
  • Clinical Partnerships: Clinical pathway co-design, shared evaluation metrics, and data governance agreements.
  • Technology Contributors: API integrators and tool developers aligned with open licensing.

Contribution Guidelines

  1. Submit intent via collaboration intake form detailing scope and data needs.
  2. Align on contribution charter specifying deliverables, review cadence, and publication plans.
  3. Maintain transparent documentation and adhere to repository governance policies.

Future Enhancements

  • Automated onboarding workflow with contributor agreements and dataset catalog access.
  • Public activity log indexing collaborative submissions and approvals.
  • Integration with ORCID and institutional identity providers for attribution.
